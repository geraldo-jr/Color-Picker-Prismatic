"Since the 1800s combinations of red, green, and blue lights have often been used in psychology experiments to mix colors, and the proportional weights of those primary lights have been used to represent the colors as a triangle, often called a Maxwell Color Triangle." - PETER SHIRLEY & DAVID HART

Use the tool below to convert RGB to ρ, ɣ, β and L values and have the proportional weight of the primary lights.
